## Competitive Analysis (5 Competitors)

### 1. Zapier - Market Leader
- **Pricing:** $19.99-$750+/mo (usage scales aggressively)
- **Strengths:** 8K integrations, brand recognition
- **Weaknesses:** **Pricing alienating users** (confirmed by evidence)

### 2. n8n - Open Source
- **Pricing:** FREE (self-host) OR $0-$50+/mo (cloud)
- **Strengths:** Free, powerful, developer-friendly
- **Weaknesses:** **Learning curve barrier** (confirmed by evidence - "getting stuck")

### 3. Make.com
- **Pricing:** $9-$29/mo (operations-based)
- **Strengths:** Cheaper than Zapier, visual workflows
- **Weaknesses:** "Operations run out quickly", pricing confusing

### 4. Pipedream
- **Pricing:** FREE tier + usage credits
- **Strengths:** Developer-friendly, generous free tier
- **Weaknesses:** **Developer-focused** (not for no-code users)

### 5. ActivePieces
- **Pricing:** FREE (self-host) or cloud tiers
- **Strengths:** Open source, easier than n8n
- **Weaknesses:** Less mature, smaller ecosystem

**Gap Identified:** Space between n8n (free but hard) and Zapier (easy but expensive). Make tries but pricing still confusing. White space for **affordable AND easy**.
